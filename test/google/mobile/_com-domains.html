Description

The domain name .com is a top-level domain in the Domain Name System of the Internet. Added at the beginning of 1985, its name is derived from the word commercial, indicating its original intended purpose for domains registered by commercial organizations. Later, the domain opened for general purposes. Wikipedia
Registry: Verisign
Actual use: Used for general purposes and is widely regarded as the standard for TLDs
Intended use: Commercial entities
Introduced: January 1, 1985; 38 years ago
